# Types of sensors:
 ## Moisture sensor (Index in article: 3.1):
  -Main job:
	Measurement of soil's characteristics to indicate water
	content.
  -Content measured:
	1-Dielctric permittivity (capaticance): How much electricity
	the siol can store.
	2-Matrix potential (Tension): How hard the plant roots can
	drink water from the soil.
	3-Volumetric moisture (VWC) : How much of water in specific
	amount of soil.
  -components:
	1-Probe electrodes insede soil to deliver "bias" and "readout".
	2-Communication circuites: e.g: coaxial cables.
	3-Electronic circuites: for signale execution.
  -Types (based on operational systems):
	1-Capacitive-based: stores amount of energy based on water
	amount in soil (measure capatitance).

 ## Water flow sensor:
  -Main job:
	Measures the flow cantity of water(Litres) per minute.
  -Components:
	A small wheel inside of a plastic sheild that spins with the
	flow of water.

 ## Water level sensor:
  -Main job:
	Measures the level of water in a tank.
  -Components:
	An ultrasonic device that sends waves and records the time
	until it comes back, this is used to calculate the volume of
	the water tank.

 ## Air temperature/humidity:
  -Main job:
	Measures air temperature and humidity.
